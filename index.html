About This Tool

A simple, free converter for Base64 encoding and decoding images. Perfect for developers, designers, and anyone working with Base64 data.

Supported Formats:

Input: PNG, JPEG, GIF, WebP, BMP

Output: PNG, JPEG, WebP

How It Works

Base64 encoding converts binary image data into text format, making it easy to embed images in HTML, CSS, or JSON.

All processing happens in your browser - no data is sent to any server, ensuring complete privacy and instant results.
